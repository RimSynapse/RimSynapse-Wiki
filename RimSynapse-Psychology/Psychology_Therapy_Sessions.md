# Therapy Sessions

RimSynapse-Psychology allows colonists to console each other and work through grief or trauma via player-driven **Therapy Sessions**.

## Initiating Therapy
You can direct a drafted or undrafted colonist to initiate a therapy session with another awake colonist by right-clicking them and selecting **Initiate Therapy Session**.

**Acceptance Logic:**
- Target pawns can refuse the session if they actively dislike the initiator (Opinion < -20) or if they are currently performing a critical job.
- Slaves and Prisoners cannot refuse therapy sessions from colonists.

## The Session UI
Once both pawns meet, a floating UI window opens. This interface gives you full control over how the therapy is conducted:

- **Guiding Hand (Manual Mode)**: Enable this toggle to manually type out what the initiating pawn says. The target pawn's response will be generated by the LLM based on their mood and ideology.
- **Resolve in Background (Auto Mode)**: Disable the Guiding Hand and click "Resolve in Background". The UI will close, and the two pawns will naturally converse. The initiator's dialogue will be automatically generated by the LLM, taking into account their **Intelligence**, **Social (Charisma)**, and **Ideology**.
- **Watch (Auto Mode with UI)**: You can leave the window open in Auto Mode to watch the conversation play out in real-time as the game ticks.

At the end of the session, both pawns will generate an opportunistic memory about the conversation, which will affect their Trust and Familiarity metrics.
